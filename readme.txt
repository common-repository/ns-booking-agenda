== Description ==

NS Booking Agenda is a plugin for dentists, lawyers, plumbers and all professional who sell his service hourly. You can set your work week (from monday to sunday) and your daily work hours (from 00:00 to 24:00). Set your hourly price and Ns Booking Agenda will do the rest. Your customer can check their reservations inside MyAccount section. Also they can ask to remove their own booking thanks to a contact form inside MyAccount.

**PLUGIN FEATURES**
* set your work week
* set your daily work hours
* set your hourly price
* customer can check their booking inside MyAccount page
* customer can ask to remove their own reservation
* you receive immediatly the payment thanks to PayPal
* Outlook integration (NEW FEATURES)
* WooCommerce 3.0 ready

*We test our plugins with many other. If you find incompatibility with one specific plugin, please inform us at <a href="mailto:report@nsthemes.com">report@nsthemes.com</a>.*

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire 'ns-booking-agenda' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. View admin menu, then click on 'Settings' -> 'NS Booking Agenda'


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is required WooCommerce to use this plugin? =

Yes WooCommerce is required to use this plugin.

= My Agenda Calendar display 404 error, what can I do? =

Simply you have to go in Settings->Permalink and click on save.

= Uninstall this plugin will clean my databse? =

Yes, if you uninstall it, all data will be lost.
